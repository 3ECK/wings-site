DAOs are the future

Focus on your business!

Wings provides the complete required framework for your future DAO. Save time, save money.

Revenues as shares or currency

As a holder of DAO tokens, you are eligible to receive a portion of all revenues from future campaigns.

Simple set-up & management

With our simple to use web platform, you will have your DAO up and running in no time. Additionally, our IM Bots will allow you to control your DAO, reach the crowds and save time and resources

Easy investment management

Wings brings the power to the investors. All backed projects are accessible using the web application and IM bots. Back a project, create a vote or write a review - all from the palm of your hand

More likely to succeed

All new DAO projects are submitted for community review before being published. Get feedback on your project and increase your chances for a successful campaign

Trusted community

Token owners are entitled to vote, according to their shares, on any new DAO submitted to the platform. The decision which campaigns get listed is in your hands

Transparency & Investors security

A DAO protects you and your investors. Blockchain transparency and the secure investment nature of DAOs will provide a more successful campaign in comparison to traditional ICOs

Control & Transparency

Your investment is secured by the nature of the DAO and the transparency of the blockchain. The core of the platform is open source and the contract, commits and advancement are visible to all

IM Bots HUB

No new applications ! Whether you use Telegram, Whatsapp or Facebook Messenger - On-boarding, affiliate marketing, project backing, DAO management and more - All available using simple convex UX
