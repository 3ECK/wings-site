Sharing The Rewards

DAO token owners are eligible to receive a portion of all revenues from future campaigns.

Security & Transparency

DAO ownership tokens are secured by blockchain technologies and smart contracgs. The control of the DAO remains in the hands of the owners. The core of the Wings platform is open source with the smart contracts, commits and advancement visible on secure public ledgers such as Ethereum and Bitcoin.

Establishing a Trusted Community

DAO owners are entitled to vote, according to their portion, on any new DAO submitted to the Wings platform. The decision which projecgs get listed are in the hands of the DAO community.

Easy Management By DAO Supporters

Wings brings the power back to project supporters. All backed projects are accessible using Web applications and IM bots. Back a project, create a vote or write a review - all without writing one line of code.

Complete DAO Framework

Wings provides a complete framework for DAOs formation, management and communications.

Simple Set-up & Management

With Wings, DAOs are up and running in no time. Wings IM Bots save time and resources by bringing full control of DAOs and DAO user interactions.

Increasing Success Rates

All DAO projects under community review before being publish gaining feedback and followers that can make for a successful DAO formation.

Transparency & Security
